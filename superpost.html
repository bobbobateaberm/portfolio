SuperPost

SuperPost is a post-based website that emerged from a problem at school.The website was created to solve the problem of expiring media/files in a group chat. When quiz time came, recent homework files were often gone, so I made a solution: a free website where files never expire.

The website includes a Login/Registration system, a Room system, and a Post system. Users can only delete their own posts or rooms, not those of others.
